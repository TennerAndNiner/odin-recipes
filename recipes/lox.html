Lox Bagel

Description

A lox bagel sandwich is a sandwich with lox smoked salmon, cream cheese, bagel and other vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Bagels
  • Cream Cheese
  • Lox Smoked Salmon
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion

Steps

  1. First, smear your bagel with cream cheese
  2. Place the lox on your bagel
  3. Add tomatoes and onions
  4. Serve!
