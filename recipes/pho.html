PHO

Description

Pho is a famous Vietnamese dish. You can find Pho all around the world where the Vietnamese diaspora is found. It usually contains rice noodles, broth, a meat of your choice and vegetable toppings.

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodles
  • Beef
  • Cilantro
  • Green Onion
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Drain your rice noodles
  2. Cook your beef broth with beef and onion
  3. Place the rice noodles in a bowl
  4. Place your meat of choice on the rice noodles
  5. Add Vegetable Toppings
  6. Pour Broth
  7. Serve!
