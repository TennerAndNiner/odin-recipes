Steak

Description

A basic steak that can be cooked for your satisfaction!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season your steak with salt and pepper
  2. Wait 20 minutes
  3. Add oil and butter to your pan
  4. Heat the pan to medium to high
  5. Place the steak sideways
  6. Cook the steak until the steak is medium rare
  7. Whilst cooking, base the steak with butter
  8. Place the steak onto a warm plate and serve!
